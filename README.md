# 📊 Marketing Mix Modeling & Budget Optimization Dashboard

## Overview

🚀 This project is an end-to-end Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) and Budget Optimization solution built in Power BI. The dashboard was designed to simulate how organizations can monitor marketing performance, diagnose channel efficiency, forecast revenue outcomes, and optimize budget allocation using scenario analysis.

The solution combines:

- Executive KPI reporting
- Marketing channel diagnostics
- Revenue trend analysis
- Time intelligence modeling
- Rolling revenue analysis
- Interactive what-if scenario modeling
- Forecasted revenue simulation
- Marginal ROI optimization
- Business recommendation logic

The dashboard demonstrates how business intelligence can support strategic marketing investment decisions through data-driven analysis and executive storytelling.

---

## 🧩 Business Problem

Marketing teams often struggle to answer critical business questions such as:

- **Which marketing channels generate the strongest ROI?**
- **Which acquisition channels are inefficient?**
- **How should marketing budget be reallocated?**
- **What is the projected impact of increasing or reducing spend by channel?**
- **Which channels generate the highest marginal return?**

This dashboard was built to simulate a practical business intelligence solution capable of:

- **Monitoring executive marketing KPIs**
- **Diagnosing inefficient acquisition channels**
- **Evaluating customer acquisition costs**
- **Forecasting revenue performance**
- **Simulating budget optimization scenarios**
- **Supporting strategic marketing allocation decisions**

## 🔍Key Business Insights

### Executive Performance Insights

- Revenue performance remained stable despite fluctuations in monthly growth.
- Email and Referral channels consistently generated the strongest ROI performance.
- CAC performance varied significantly across acquisition channels.

### Diagnostic Insights

- LinkedIn demonstrated elevated acquisition costs relative to revenue contribution.
- Google maintained scalable acquisition efficiency.
- Facebook generated moderate performance but weaker efficiency relative to spend.

### Optimization Insights

- Incremental spend allocation toward Email and Referral produced the strongest projected marginal return.
- Budget reductions across weaker-performing channels improved portfolio-level efficiency.
- Scenario analysis demonstrated measurable revenue lift opportunities through optimized allocation.
